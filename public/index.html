Behind the work

From digital model to physical artifact.

Furious Facades is led by designer and maker Josh Rudloff, drawing on more than 20 years of 3D modeling experience, including work for theme park rides, sculpture, ride cars, and animatronics. Today that experience powers a hands-on studio creating wearable creatures and unusual objects with family and fellow craftspeople.
